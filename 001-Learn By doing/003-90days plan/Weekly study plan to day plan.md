
## Prompt:

```
You are an expert in the topic I provide.
You are also an expert at creating structured learning plans for job-ready skills.

I will give you my weekly learning plan.

Your task is to:

1. Carefully understand my weekly plan
2. Create a clear 6-day daily plan from it
3. Focus on practical learning, not theory overload
4. Apply the 80/20 rule — select the 20% of topics that will give 80% real-world job results
5. Make the plan job-switch focused

Structure the 6 days like this:

* Day 1–2: Strong fundamentals and core concepts (only what is truly important)
* Day 3–4: Hands-on practice and real-world exercises
* Day 5–6: Build a small practical project using what I learned

For each day include:

* Topics to learn
* What to practice
* What to build (if applicable)
* Tools required
* Expected outcome

Keep the plan:

* Simple
* Practical
* No unnecessary theory
* Focused on job-readiness
* Clear and structured

Wait for my weekly plan before generating the daily plan.
```
