
###  What is networking?
* Computer network is a group of computers and devices that are used to share information and resources
* Transport system of data

### Types of network?
1. PAN -> Personal area --> Bluetooth
2. LAN --> Local --> wifi
3. MAN --> metropolitan --> city cable
4. WAN --> wide area --> Internet

### Routes vs Switch vs Hub?
1. Hub:
	1. It's broadcast to all connected device
2. Switch:
	1. It will communicate with one device
3. Router:
	1. it communicate with outside network
