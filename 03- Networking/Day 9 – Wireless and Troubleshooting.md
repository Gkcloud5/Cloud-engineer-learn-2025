### 9.1 wifi  basics:
* SSID: Wifi network name
* Frequency band: 
	* 2.4GHZ --> long range, slower speed
	* 5GHZ --> Shorter range, faster speed

### Tools for network engineer:
1. ping:
	1. Check reachability
	2. output
		1. ms(time)
		2. packet loss
		3. successful and failed replies
2. Traceroute:
	1. shows the path
	2. Helps where find slowdown or failure happens
